The Evidence

We rebuilt the whole ledger — and it runs.

Not a manifesto about what national accounts should measure. A working balance sheet for 200-plus countries and 3,000-plus US counties, one identity, 75 years — with every number carrying its own honesty label.

  • A complete national balance sheet — CW = PA + NC + HR + SI — for 200+ countries, 1950–2025. Production
  • 7,360 administrative roots across 237 countries — 97.6% of world population — and a 38× measurement asymmetry. Experimental
  • A county-level finding: the opportunity to participate tracks hidden wealth better than participation itself. Experimental

Take-Off in Convergence Terms

Production

Put 240 countries on one axis against the US frontier, and the Bretton-Woods record reads very differently: of ~46 countries that peaked under the fixed-rate system, 42 have fallen back below 30% of the US frontier — a 91% non-convergence rate.

Capacity, Not Uptake

Experimental

Take the trust-infrastructure residual to the US county and it splits cleanly: the opportunity to participate moves with hidden wealth; realized participation moves against it — exactly what the theory predicts if a substantive right is a liberty.
