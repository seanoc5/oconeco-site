# Source agency directory

FAND deliberately cherry-picks a small number of key indicators from each source agency. The point is to let users **leave the site to verify** — and come back. Every published value records the agency, table, year, and a direct URL to the original data.

## International sources (country-level data)

| Agency | Scope | URL |
|---|---|---|
| **WDI** — World Bank World Development Indicators | GNI Atlas, GDP, population, education indicators | [data.worldbank.org](https://data.worldbank.org/) |
| **IMF** — International Monetary Fund | Balance of Payments (BOP), International Investment Position (IIP), Government Finance Statistics (GFS), World Economic Outlook (WEO) | [imf.org/data](https://www.imf.org/en/Data) |
| **FAO** — Food and Agriculture Organization (FAOSTAT) | Agricultural land, forestry, fisheries | [fao.org/faostat](https://www.fao.org/faostat/) |
| **USGS** — US Geological Survey | Mineral Commodity Summaries (global reserves and production for 31 commodities) | [usgs.gov/centers/national-minerals-information-center](https://www.usgs.gov/centers/national-minerals-information-center) |
| **EIA** — US Energy Information Administration | Global energy reserves and production (fossil fuels) | [eia.gov/international](https://www.eia.gov/international/) |
| **OECD** — Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development | Productivity, fixed capital stock, depreciation | [stats.oecd.org](https://stats.oecd.org/) |
| **UN WPP** — UN Population Division, World Population Prospects | Population, working-age share, life expectancy | [population.un.org/wpp](https://population.un.org/wpp/) |
| **BIS** — Bank for International Settlements | Locational banking statistics, cross-border flows | [bis.org/statistics](https://www.bis.org/statistics/) |

## The UX pattern

Every data value on a country / state / county page carries a small source marker. Click it → see the agency, table name, year, and a direct URL to the original data. **Not a separate bibliography page** — inline, contextual, and immediate.

The four-axis architecture treats *Source* as a first-class navigation axis: every number on the site has a verifiable provenance chain back to a public agency.
