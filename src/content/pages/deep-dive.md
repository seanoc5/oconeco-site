# Deep Dive

This section is **password-protected** and intended for named institutional reviewers — World Bank alumni, IARIW reviewers, academic peers, development agency leadership. Access is tracked.

If you have not received a password, the public-facing material in [Evidence](/evidence/), [Framework](/framework/), and [Methodology](/methodology/) is the appropriate entry point.

## What's behind the gate

- **Full working papers** — Appendix E (FAND vs CWoN comparative analysis), Thin Description of FAND (long form), First Principles of FAND Accounting, FAND Long Haul (five-scale architecture)
- **Downloadable workbooks** — every balance-sheet component (PA, NC, HR, SI, SR, DM, CBA, CBL) per country, plus US state and county tables
- **Full Operational Guideline documents** — OG-001 through OG-010 in `.docx` and `.md`
- **Complete replication package** — the 128 builder scripts, requirements, SHA-256 checksums, MANIFEST documentation, and SQLite cache files for both TL1 and US bottom-up

## Phase 1 vs Phase 2

- **Phase 1 (mid-July 2026):** Shared password. One credential emailed to each reviewer alongside the link. No per-reviewer access tracking.
- **Phase 2 (Q3 2026):** Individual accounts. Magic-link email per reviewer. Tracks who opened what — answers questions like "did Haishan's team look at the workbooks?"

See issue [#6](https://github.com/seanoc5/oconeco-site/issues/6) for the decision in progress.
