# US counties

3,258 counties. Same balance-sheet identity. At this scale, the cross-border story is **commuter flows** — income earned in one county, spent in another. The economic reality most Americans actually live.

## Why counties matter

A bedroom suburb and its downtown neighbor look almost identical on a GDP map — *production* is captured where the work happens. But on a *wealth* map they diverge sharply: Manhattan accumulates produced assets and financial wealth; Bergen County accumulates *household* wealth (housing, education, retirement claims) that the Manhattan-earned income built.

You cannot see this dynamic at the country scale. You cannot see it at the state scale. You can only see it when the unit of measurement is small enough to *be on one side or the other* of a commuter flow.
