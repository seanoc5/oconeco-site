# Global Leaves crosstab

The flagship result. Every administrative unit FAND tracks — 7,101 of them across 237 countries — placed in a 5×5 grid by the structure of its wealth.

## What the crosstab shows

The world's wealth distribution measured *by what kind of capital* dominates each place. The headline finding: **a 38× measurement asymmetry** between how much wealth conventional accounting captures vs how much FAND captures, driven by the substantive-rights side of the balance sheet (HR + SR) that nobody else measures at scale.
