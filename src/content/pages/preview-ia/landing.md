# GDP tells you what a country *earns*. We measure what it *owns*.

200+ countries · 50 US states · 3,258 US counties · 1950–2025

The full balance sheet — not just income, but everything a place *owns*, including the things nobody else measures.

## Why this site exists

GDP tells you what a country *earned* last year. The balance sheet tells you what it *has* — and what it owes.

OconEco's FAND project computes a complete national balance sheet for 200+ countries from 1950–2025, plus US state and county-level extensions. The identity is `CW = PA + NC + HR + SI`:

- **PA** — Produced Assets (factories, roads, equipment)
- **NC** — Natural Capital (oil, minerals, fisheries, farmland, forests)
- **HR** — Human Resources (the value of an educated, healthy population)
- **SI** — Social Infrastructure (institutional trust, rule of law, the things that make a society work)

Most of the world's wealth is in the last two. **Nobody else measures them at this scale. We do.**
